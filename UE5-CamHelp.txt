CAMERA CONTROL GUIDE
BINDININGS AND SHORTCUTS
-----------------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------   
-----------------------------------------------------------------


----------------------------------
Camera Movement
----------------------------------

WASD/Arrow Keys: Move the camera forward, backward, left, and right.
Shift + W: Move the camera forward (faster than WASD)
Shift + S: Move the camera backward (faster than WASD)
Shift + A: Move the camera left (faster than WASD)
Shift + D: Move the camera right (faster than WASD)
Ctrl + W: Move the camera up
Ctrl + S: Move the camera down
Ctrl + A: Move the camera left (slower than WASD)
Ctrl + D: Move the camera right (slower than WASD)


-----------------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------
Camera Zoom
-------------------------

Mouse Wheel: Zoom the camera in and out.
Ctrl + Mouse Wheel: Zoom the camera in and out more slowly and precisely.
Shift + Mouse Wheel: Zoom the camera in and out more quickly.
Ctrl + Shift + Mouse Wheel: Zoom the camera in and out (more slowly and precisely)


-----------------------------------------------------------------
-------------------------------------------------------
Camera Rotation and Orbiting
-------------------------------------------------------

RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Orbit the camera around the pivot point (default is the selected object or the center of the screen).
RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Shift + Drag: Pan the camera horizontally and vertically.
Shift + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Pan the camera horizontally and vertically (alternative to Ctrl + RMB)
Ctrl + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Dolly the camera forward and backward.
Ctrl + Shift + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Rotate the camera (rotate the camera around its own axis).
Alt + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Snap the camera to a specific angle (e.g., 90 degrees) while orbiting.
Alt + LMB (Left-Mouse Button) + Drag: Rotate the camera around the horizontal axis.
Alt + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Rotate the camera around the vertical axis.
Ctrl + Alt + LMB (Left-Mouse Button) + Drag: Rotate the camera around the depth axis (roll).


-----------------------------------------------------------------
-------------------------------------------------
Camera Pivot and Position
------------------------------------------------

Spacebar: Toggle the camera's pivot point between the selected object and the center of the screen.
Spacebar + LMB (Left-Mouse Button) + Drag: Move the camera's pivot point.
Spacebar + RMB (Right-Mouse Button) + Drag: Move the camera's pivot point (alternative to Spacebar + LMB).
Home: Toggle the camera to its default position and orientation.


-----------------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------
Camera Modes and Projection
--------------------------------------------------------

F1: Switch to Camera mode.
F2: Switch to Orthographic camera mode.
F3: Switch to Isometric camera mode.
F4: Switch to Camera Lock mode (locks the camera to the current orientation).
F5: Switch to Camera Unlock mode (unlocks the camera from its current orientation).
End: Toggle the camera's orthographic projection on and off.


-----------------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Camera Bookmarks and Screenshots
-----------------------------------------------------------------

Ctrl + Shift + F1-F12: Save and recall camera bookmarks (up to 12 bookmarks)
Ctrl + F1-F12: Load saved camera bookmarks.
F11: Toggle the camera's HUD (heads-up display) on and off.
F12: Take a screenshot of the current camera view.


-----------------------------------------------------------------
-------------------------
Miscellaneous
-------------------------

Ctrl + 0: Reset the camera's zoom to its default value.
Ctrl + Shift + F: Toggle the camera's frame rate display on and off.
Page Up/Page Down: Move the camera up and down (in orthographic projection mode only).
